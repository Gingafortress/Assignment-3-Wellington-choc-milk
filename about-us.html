About Us

The Wellington Chocolate Milkers is social club that was founded in April 2015. The WCM club is a fortnightly social gathering of those in Wellington that find they require a higher quality chocolate milk.

At the fortnightly meetings, the club will drink the only high quality chocolate milk on the market so far: Lewis Creamery Road Chocolate Milk. They will also test members attempts at making their own high quality chocolate milk; which will then be rated and if deemed worthy will be placed on thewebsite as a recommended recipe.

Trips to dairies and chocolate makers are organised every two months, so that members can educate themselves on where there milky goodness is coming from.

It's a time and a place to hang out with friends and get drunk on the chocolate and the milk.
