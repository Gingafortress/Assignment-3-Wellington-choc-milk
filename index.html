Welcome to the Wellington Chocolate Milkers on the internet. The only social group in New Zealand that focuses on chocolate milk. Every fortnight we gather to drink chocolate and milk, testing concoctions that our members have created. We judge popular brands of milk and grade and recommend them. So if you would enjoy a social gathering and consuming choclate milk in its varied forms; sign-up today and we’ll see you at the next meeting every fortnight.
